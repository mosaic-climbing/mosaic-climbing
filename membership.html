What sets us apart…

There are many wonderful options in the Cincinnati metro for those seeking social and physical outlets. We encourage you to explore the wide variety of opportunities available in order to find the best fit for you.

  • Most affordable full-service indoor climbing facility
  • Most climbing terrain - over 50% more
  • Largest selection of routes and boulders
  • Lowest cost per square foot of climbing surface
  • Highest, longest, and steepest lead climbing terrain
  • Most autobelays (totalling 10)
  • Only facility offering complimentary Learn to Lead classes
  • Complimentary monthly member workshops
  • Complimentary monthly member appreciation events
  • Only facility with no-fees. Ever. (Start-up, cancellation, freeze, etc.)
  • Only facility with 24-hr access option
  • Rentals, top rope classes, and lead/top-rope competency checks included in all day passes and memberships
  • All member guests receive $5 off their day pass
  • Fully equipped, independent fitness facility - no chalk dust!
  • Treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers
  • Squat racks, trap bars, hex barbells
  • Kettlebells, dumbbells, med balls, plyo boxes
  • Cable machines, TRX systems
  • Various recovery, mobility, and balance tools
  • Independent yoga studio
  • Complimentary fitness and yoga classes
  • Co-working spaces and conference rooms
  • Very family-friendly with ample youth terrain
  • A giant parking lot where parking is essentially never an issue
