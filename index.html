Mosaic.

Your body was designed to climb. Explore with us.

At Mosaic we are committed to creating a space and challenges that allow you to explore climbing in a way that works for you. A stagnant, impersonal gym environment is no fun. Your mind and body deserve a challenge.

Bouldering

A unique style of climbing done over a padded floor without ropes or harnesses. You can boulder with or without a partner.

Learn about bouldering

Top-rope, auto-belay & lead

Top rope and auto belay climbing are done with a rope suspended over head — top-roping needs a partner to belay, auto belays don't. Lead climbing is advanced and starts with the rope at the bottom, clipping to progressive protection points as you ascend.

Learn about ropes

Fitness & yoga

A large independent fitness center and a separate yoga studio with no chalk dust. Treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers, squat racks, kettlebells, plyo boxes, cable machines, TRX. Recovery, mobility, balance.
