Pricing

How our pricing works.

All group packages begin at a base rate of $35 per person and scale based on three key factors:

  1. Group size. Larger groups benefit from volume discounts.
  2. Duration. Standard sessions range from 2 to 3 hours, but can extend to full days upon request.
  3. Resource allotment. Premium tiers include dedicated instructors, facilitators, exclusive facility access, and other amenities.
Included

What every group receives.

  • Safety orientation and basic equipment instruction for all participants
  • All necessary climbing equipment (harness, shoes)
  • Dedicated check-in and group coordination upon arrival
  • Access to restrooms, water stations, and common areas
  • Liability waivers processed digitally in advance to streamline arrival
  • In general, most groups are expected to be relatively self-sufficient. If more 1-on-1 assistance is requested, please let us know in advance. Fees apply.

Availability

When to book.

  • Booking availability is largely dependent upon peak hours and staff availability. For small groups, we can generally accommodate with at least 7–10 days of notice. For larger groups of 20 or more, we recommend booking two or more weeks in advance.
  • Groups can also inquire to book outside of regular business hours. Fees may apply.
